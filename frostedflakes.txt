Ingredients:
1 bowl
1 spoon
1 Milk (at least 8oz)
1 Box of Frosted Flakes (not stale)

Directions:
1. Set bowl on table
2. Open cereal and pour into bowl
3. Open milk and pour over cereal
4. Insert spoon and enjoy.


WARNING!!! DO NOT ADD MILK FIRST!!!
That is weird.  You will make splashes and not properly soak cereal in milk.
People who add milk first are a menace to society and have no friends.
